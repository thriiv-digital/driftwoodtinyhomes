FAQ

Good questions.

Do I need council approval?

Most of our homes are built on a registered trailer, which generally sits outside standard planning approval — though your council and site may have specific rules. We help you check before we start.

How long does a build take?

Typically 10–14 weeks from signed contract, depending on the model and customisations. We'll give you a firm date and stick to it.

Can I customise a floor plan?

Always. Our three models are starting points — we regularly adjust room layouts, decks, and finishes to suit how you live.

Do you deliver outside Shellharbour?

Yes — we regularly deliver across the Illawarra, South Coast, and Southern Highlands, and further afield on request.

What's included in the price?

Trailer, full build, fit-out, appliances, and delivery within the Illawarra. Solar, off-grid systems, and premium finishes are quoted separately so pricing stays transparent.
